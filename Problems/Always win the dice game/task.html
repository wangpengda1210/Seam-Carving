Always win the dice game

Our friends want to go to Las Vegas to win some money on slot machines, and we want to convince them that it's a bad idea and the casino always wins. To show them how it works we'll play the six-sided dice game with them. The game is simple: they roll the dice N times, then we throw it N times, and whoever got a greater sum wins the bank. Our friends believe that the random generator is fair so they don't mind us giving them a generator.

Our task is to hack the system and provide a seed for the generator that wins the game for a given integer number of steps N. Let's try different seeds until we find the one that guarantees our victory, and then return the seed. Remember: our friends roll the six-sided dice first N times, and then it's our turn for the next N times.

Once we see that the seed works, we need to create a new Random with that seed since we can't roll back the existing one.
